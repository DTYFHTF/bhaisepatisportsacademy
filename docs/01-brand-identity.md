# 01 — Brand Identity

## Brand Essence

**Name:** Bhaisepati Sports Academy
**Short Name:** BSA
**Tagline:** *Train harder. Move faster. Grow stronger.*
**Origin:** Bhaisepati, Kathmandu, Nepal
**Category:** Sports Training / Athletic Development

BSA is a community-centered sports training facility dedicated to developing athletes at every level. Badminton is the heart of the academy — the owner's passion and the most popular activity on campus. The name is tied to the community it serves: Bhaisepati, a neighborhood in Lalitpur where athletes come to train, compete, and grow.

---

## Positioning

### What BSA Is
- A professional sports training facility for all levels — beginner to competitive
- Badminton-first, with supporting gym and recovery facilities
- A community where athletes train, grow, compete, and belong
- Youth-focused and family-friendly

### What BSA Is Not
- A casual recreation center with courts on the side
- A gym that happens to have a badminton court
- Luxury-oriented or exclusive
- A one-size-fits-all fitness club

### Target Audience
- Youth athletes aged 10–22 and their parents
- Competitive badminton players seeking structured coaching
- Adults who want regular sport and fitness activity
- Complete beginners curious about badminton for the first time

---

## Core Facilities

### Badminton Courts
- Professional playing surface
- Proper lighting for training and competition
- The primary identity of the academy

### Gym & Strength Training
- Athletic conditioning tailored to court sports
- Strength development for injury prevention and performance
- Available to academy members

### Sauna & Recovery
- Muscle recovery after intense training sessions
- Post-session relaxation for members

---

## Programs & Academy Structure

### Badminton Programs

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **Foundation** | Absolute beginners. Basic technique, court rules, footwork. |
| **Intermediate Development** | Stroke refinement, rally training, match play. |
| **Advanced & Competitive** | Tournament preparation, fitness integration, strategy. |
| **Youth Academy** | Age-specific programs for junior players under 16. |
| **Open Play** | Unstructured court time for enrolled members. |

### Supporting Programs
- **Fitness & Conditioning** — Athletic fitness designed for court sport performance.
- **Recovery Sessions** — Sauna and guided recovery for intensive training members.

## Visual Identity

### Color Direction
- **Energetic and dynamic** — visual identity mirrors the speed and precision of badminton
- **Bold, athletic** — strong headlines, competitive feel
- **Youth-facing** — bright, clear, high contrast

| Role | Direction |
|---|---|
| Background | Clean, near-white |
| Primary Text | Near black |
| Brand Accent | Energetic primary color (to be defined in design system) |
| Secondary | Deep navy or strong complementary |
| Muted | Medium gray |

> See [04 — Design System](04-design-system.md) for finalized token values.

### Typography
- **Primary:** Inter — legible at all sizes, clean, modern
- **Display:** Strong sans-serif for headings (athletic feel)
- Motion: subtle fade-in-up animations (200–300ms), no bounce

## Brand Voice

### Tone Principles

| Principle | Means | Does Not Mean |
|---|---|---|
| **Energetic** | Motivating, forward-moving, action-first | Hyperbolic or loud |
| **Direct** | Clear CTAs, no fluff | Blunt or cold |
| **Inclusive** | Welcomes all levels, ages, backgrounds | Generic and forgettable |
| **Competitive** | Celebrates progress and achievement | Intimidating for beginners |
| **Nepali** | Rooted in local community | Exotic or tourist-facing |

### Copy Examples

**Hero headline:**
> *Train harder. Move faster. Grow stronger.*

**Enrollment CTA:**
> *Join BSA — start your journey today.*

**Program page sub-header:**
> *Badminton Foundation Program* — not "Discover your inner champion"

**Court booking:**
> *Reserve your court. Play today.*

**Empty sessions state:**
> *No sessions today — check back tomorrow.*

**Enrollment confirmed:**
> *You're in. We'll see you on the court.*

### What To Avoid
- Generic sports clichés: "unleash your potential", "elevate your game"
- Overpromising to beginners
- Intimidating language that excludes recreational players
- Corporate tone — BSA is a community, not a brand

## What "Bhaisepati" Means for This Brand

BSA is rooted in its neighborhood. Being from Bhaisepati means:
- **Built for this community** — the families and youth of Bhaisepati and surrounding areas
- **Accessible** — priced for Nepali families, not elite clubs
- **Local pride** — a facility the neighborhood can point to and be proud of
- **Proven** — the owner's own love of badminton is the foundation
